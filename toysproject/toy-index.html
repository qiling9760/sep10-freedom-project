What are Toys and Puzzles

Welcome to our website, in this website we will briefly provide information about Puzzles and Toys. The history of toys and puzzles can be traced back to prehistoric times. They come in many different forms such as wood and plastic. As technology penetrates deeper into our lives, it also impacts toys and puzzles. Toys and puzzles become more delicate and advanced as technology is involved in them. This website will look at how technology has changed toys and puzzles and how it will further impact them.
